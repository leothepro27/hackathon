Don't know what the different parts of a computer mean? Here is a quick beginner's guide to learn more:

  • CPU: Stands for Central Processing Unit and it's the processor, essentially the brain, of a computer. Generally, the higher the numbers, the better the CPU.
  • RAM: Stands for Random Access Memory (can also be considered as short-term memory). The more RAM, the better, because the computer can then execute more processes at once efficiently.
  • GPU: Stands for Graphics Processing Unit. This refers to the part in the computer that is responsible for the graphics in videos and games.
  • Screen (Resolution): When looking at the screen, you might see something like this: 2560x1600 (or something slightly different). This refers to the screen's resolution. The higher the numbers, the more pixels. The more pixels, the sharper and more detailed your screen will look like.
